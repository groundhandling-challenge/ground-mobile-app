Groundhandling Challenge

Groundhandling Challenge was started by Portuguese paragliding pilot André Bandarra with the objective to get more people into Groundhandling, create a community around it and have fun in the process. He crafted the content and released the project’s website in December 2017.

In the spirit of André’s objective, German paragliding pilot Michael Schmidt-Voigt joined the adventure and created this accompanying app.
